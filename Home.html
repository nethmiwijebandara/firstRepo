Here at Smiling Pups, we specialize in bringing joy.
Both to the dogs who we help find forever homes, and to the humans who will love them for yeas to come.

Our pups come from individuals who were not able to keep and care for them. Our mission to find a second chance with a family that is looking to add a new member and enrich their lives. We exclusively use foster families to house our pups, allowing us to put all of our resources towards adoption and pet education.
